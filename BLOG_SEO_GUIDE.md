## üìù Meta Description Guidelines

### Current Issues

- Some descriptions are too short (<120 characters)
- Optimal length: **120-160 characters**

### How to Write Better Meta Descriptions

**Formula:**

```
[Hook] + [Value Proposition] + [Call to Action]
```

**Examples:**

**Bad (too short):**

```
Learn about digital marketing.
(32 characters)
```

**Good:**

```
Discover the top 10 digital marketing strategies that will boost your online presence and drive more traffic to your website in 2026.
(138 characters)
```

**Template:**

```
[Action Verb] + [Specific Benefit] + [Target Audience] + [Timeframe/Context]

Examples:
- "Discover how to..."
- "Learn the secrets of..."
- "Master the art of..."
- "Get started with..."
- "Boost your [X] with..."
```

## üè∑Ô∏è Meta Title Optimization

### Guidelines

- **Length:** 50-60 characters (optimal)
- **Include:** Primary keyword + brand name
- **Format:** `[Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Keyword] - [Brand]`

**Examples:**

**Before:**

```
Digital Marketing
(18 characters - too short)
```

**After:**

```
Digital Marketing Course in Mohali | Digital Study School
(57 characters - optimal)
```

**Templates:**

```
1. [Topic] Guide: [Specific Focus] | [Brand]
2. [Number] [Topic] Tips for [Audience] - [Brand]
3. How to [Action] with [Topic] | [Brand]
4. [Topic] Course in [Location] | [Brand]
5. Best [Topic] Strategies for [Year] - [Brand]
```

## üìà Keyword Research Tips

### Finding the Right Keywords

1. **Use Google Autocomplete:**
   - Type your topic in Google
   - Note the suggested searches

2. **Check "People Also Ask":**
   - Look at related questions
   - Turn questions into keywords

3. **Analyze Competitors:**
   - Check top-ranking pages
   - Note their meta keywords

4. **Use Long-Tail Keywords:**
   - More specific = less competition
   - Example: "digital marketing course for beginners in Mohali"
